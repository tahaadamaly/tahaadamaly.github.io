Week 8: Talking Economics Homework 8

During the COVID-19 Pandemic, Denmark had a hard lockdown and Sweden did not. In the Health and Economics panel at Talking Economics, Ben Moll said that consumer spending in Sweden fell by almost as much as in Denmark indicating that lockdowns do not influence private consumption expenditure as much as the overall health crisis did. This chart uses consumption expenditure values from FRED and is rebased using a z-score for easier comparison. This shows that consumer spending in both countries fell by similar amounts showing that the lockdown by itself did not significantly influence spending.
