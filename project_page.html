Section 2: Relationship between qualifying and race position

This chart presents the correlation between the position attained during the qualifying session and the final race outcome. There are 7624 observations from the years 1996 - 2021. The correlation coefficient is 0.54 and is significant, showing that there is a positive correlation between qualifying position and race outcome. It is surprising though that the R-squared value is only 0.28 as I had expected this to be higher
